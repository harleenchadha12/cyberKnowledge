What is Cyber Security?

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and digital information from theft, damage, or unauthorized access. It involves the implementation of measures, protocols, and technologies designed to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data in the digital realm.

Cybersecurity Threats

  • Malware attacks
  • Phishing attempts
  • Ransomware
  • Website Spoofing
  • IOT Hacking
